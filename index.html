About

Geophysicist specializing in geothermal energy, hydrogeophysics, and geospatial data analytics. Driven by data-backed solutions to water and energy resource challenges. Special interests in Arctic energy systems and energy policy.

Recent graduate currently interning with the U.S. Bureau of Reclamation and incoming at the U.S. Geological Survey New York Water Science Center.

Lover of all things outdoors. I grew up near the ocean and was natural an avid beach-goer, but I have grown into chasing my love of the mountains. Geophysics has been the perfect balance of in-field and office work to continuously challenge myself.
